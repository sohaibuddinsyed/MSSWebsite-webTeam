MJCET Chat-bot

A chatbot is an artificial intelligence(AI) software that can simulate a conversation with a user in natural language through messaging applications,websites etc.This chatbot is aimed to make the marketing and registration process of studentchapters easier. This chatbot can be accessed from Facebook messenger. Students can get the complete info of any event conducted in MJCET >through this chatbot.
Chatbot also offer a way for the students to register for any desired events.




Blindness Detection:

Millions of people suffer from Diabetic Retinopathy, the leading cause of blindness among working aged adults.Detection and prevention of this disease among people living in rural areas, where medical screening is difficult to conduct, needs to be taken care of. The motive of the project is to identify potential patients and detect blindness before it happens. The objective is to create an accurate model using deep learning algorithms like convolutional neural networks which can precisely classify the severity of Diabetic Retinopathy.







Project Arena-MJCET

The goal of this project is to demonstrate the design,architecture and ambience of our college campus i.e.Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technologyby using advanced technologies like 3-dimensional modeling, Virtual reality and game engines, the projection can be divided into two parts, firstly we will design the 3D-model, then impellent virtual reality using game engine.The term “arena” refers to a specific area within a level where the player will encounter some kind of event or obstacle. Arenas are non-linear spaces, meaning theyoffer players opportunities to explore the environment. Our vision is to create a software that can be used to explore the campus using a head mounted display.
