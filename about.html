About Us

Microsoft Student Society (MSS) MJCET, started in 2017 with the goal to create leaders for tomorrow. The motto has been to Innovate, Invent and Inspire. MSS aims to help students connect with technology and make their dreams become reality. The basis of this chapter was established by a Microsoft Student Partner and ever since, it has been growing exponentially.

Mission

Microsoft Student Society is a student chapter which promote ownership, leadership in our college. The members are exposed to various upcoming technologies in order to prepare them to face various challenges. Students will learn appropriate ways to affect change, and develop their ability to interact with the adults, exchange their ideas.The purpose of Student Council is to promote pride, spirit, service, and achievement in our college. Student Council Members are a part of an elected group of leaders who stand to serve the college community.

Vision

Microsoft Student Society provides profession by providing innovative programs and services for the technological research and the development of student.MSS tries to get the students in touch with the various ideas and concepts in the ever-evolving world of technology. To keep up with the pace, MSS conducts workshops on important topics addressed by top class industry professionals. Our ideology is about a global vision to code for the better.At MSS, professionalism is prioritized, skill is fostered and ideas are invented
